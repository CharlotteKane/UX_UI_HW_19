Recent Work


On the Fly

3-week solo case study

User Research, Ideation, Wireframing

An app to make planning group travel less annoying

Over the course of this case study, research revealed that modern group traveling puts too much stress on the individual planning the trip.
This led me to designing OntheFly, an app that acts as communication hub, trip planner, and transit-tracker that groups can co-edit and maintain.
Halo Rescue

2-week group project

User Research, Storyboarding, Prototyping

A website redesign to make the adoption process coherent

Working with two other designers, I conducted a heuristic evaluation of a non-profit website.
After conducting thorough user research & stakeholder empathy, we drafted a redesign of the entire adoption flow for better transparency, discoverability, and visual unity.
